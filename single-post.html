What is the eFootball.Pro?

The eFootball.Pro is an esports tournament in which professional esports players representing real football clubs compete in 3v3 co-op matches.

The competitors are selected from among the ranks of the world’s most skilled eFootball PES players.

In the 2020-21 season, the eFootball.Pro will see representatives of the following 10 clubs duke it out for the title:

  • FC Barcelona
  • Manchester United FC
  • FC Bayern Munchen
  • Juventus
  • Arsenal FC
  • AS Roma
  • Celtic FC
  • FC Schalke 04
  • Galatasaray
  • AS Monaco

Check out last year’s tournament results here.

The eFootball.Pro is held using the eFootball PES 2021 SEASON UPDATE, developed by KONAMI.

What’s the tournament format?

The eFootball.Pro is comparised of both a league stage and a knockout stage, and is scheduled to be held between December 2020 and June 2021.

All tournament matches will be viewable on the official eFootball PES YouTube channel.
